3D Catan



I made a Settlers of Catan board on a 3D printer. The regular game board is made out of cardboard and that tends to warp with the seasons, so I made this with interlocking pieces that allow it to get knocked around a bit without destroying your game.
The files are all available on Thingiverse.

Light Wall



The Interstices dynamic light installation was a project by by employees at the JHU DMC. It is a large grid of boxes (13x5) that each contain RGB LEDS. It is equipped with various sensors to allow it to change depending on its environment.
The code is available on GitHub.

Shit For Helen



I started Shit For Helen in September 2011 as a way to show my sister neat things that I find around the internet. I try to update it once a day.

Makerbot



In the fall of 2010 I applied for and received the Creative Use of Technology Grant from the Johns Hopkins Digital Media Center so that I could create a 3D printering service for students. I chose to build a Makerbot Thing-O-Matic. I mainatain it and run the service.
