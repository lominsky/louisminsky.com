AcryLock

The AcryLock is a demonstration lock made out of acrylic and the springs from retractable pends. The lock can but laser cut from a single sheet of 1/4" acrylic, but is scalable for other widths. It was created for Project Design Lab with Danny Rozin.

Silophon

The Silophon is an assistive instrument/toy designed to create a positive experience of sound, specifically for children with autism. Silophon is designed to look like the classic xylophone, crafted with felt, allowing the user to play by hitting the keys with their hands or soft mallets, and also allowing the user to control the level of volume. It was created with Dalit Shalom as a final project for Physical Computing.

MagStripe Spoofer

I created the MagStripe Spoofer with Jeff Boye for Engineering Design Lab. It is capable of mimicing a real ID card. Additional features include reading a magnetic stripe off an existing card and storing it in memory for later use, manual entry of a value to spoof, and iterative spoofing to try multiple values.
